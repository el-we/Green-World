About Us

Green World is a non-profit environmental organization committed to addressing the urgent issues of climate change and environmental degradation. Founded in 2023, we've made it our mission to promote sustainable practices, protect the environment, and educate the public about the impact of CO2 emissions. Our team consists of dedicated environmental scientists, educators, and activists spread across the globe, working tirelessly to create a greener, more sustainable world for future generations.

Our Work

Our initiatives are focused on three primary areas: Climate Change Advocacy, Environmental Protection, and Corporate Responsibility.

Climate Change Advocacy

We actively campaign for stronger climate policies and increased investment in renewable energy. Through our advocacy work, we aim to influence decision-makers and drive systemic change to reduce global CO2 emissions.

Environmental Protection

We undertake a range of projects to protect and restore our natural environment. This includes reforestation efforts, biodiversity conservation, and protecting endangered species. We believe in the importance of maintaining the health and vitality of our planet's ecosystems for the benefit of all life on Earth.

Corporate Responsibility

We hold corporations accountable for their environmental impact. Through our Corporate Responsibility program, we encourage businesses to adopt sustainable practices, reduce their carbon footprint, and invest in green technologies. Our annual Green World Index ranks companies and countries based on their CO2 emissions, providing a transparent and accessible overview of their environmental impact.
